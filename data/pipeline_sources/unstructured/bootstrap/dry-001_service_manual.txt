Service manual excerpt — AirFlow Heat Pump Dryer 8kg (dry-001)

Model AF-HP8 · Brand AirFlow · MY2026

If the unit shows error E66 (Heat pump pressure fault), inspect the Heat Pump Loop.
Primary field failure: Heat Pump Compressor Fault. Replace Heat Pump Compressor Module (AF-HPC-8) when confirmed.
Customer may report: Clothes remain damp after full cycle.
Always disconnect power before opening the chassis.
