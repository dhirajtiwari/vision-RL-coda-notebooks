Service manual excerpt — PureRoom HEPA Air Purifier (pur-001)

Model PR-HP400 · Brand PureRoom · MY2026

If the unit shows error F02 (Filter life sensor), inspect the Filter Bay.
Primary field failure: Filter Sensor Misread. Replace Filter Presence Sensor (PR-FPS-400) when confirmed.
Customer may report: Air quality light stuck red after filter change.
Always disconnect power before opening the chassis.
