Service manual excerpt — RoboClean Stick Vacuum Pro (vac-001)

Model RC-SV90 · Brand RoboClean · MY2026

If the unit shows error E12 (Brush roll jam / high load), inspect the Agitator Drive.
Primary field failure: Brush Roll Motor Overload. Replace Brush Roll Motor Assembly (RC-BRM-90) when confirmed.
Customer may report: Loss of suction / brush roll stops.
Always disconnect power before opening the chassis.
