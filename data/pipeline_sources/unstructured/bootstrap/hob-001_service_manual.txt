Service manual excerpt — SparkLine Induction Hob 4-Zone (hob-001)

Model SL-IH4 · Brand SparkLine · MY2026

If the unit shows error E9 (Pan detection fault), inspect the Power Stage.
Primary field failure: Induction Coil Driver Fault. Replace Induction Coil Board (SL-ICB-4) when confirmed.
Customer may report: Zone flashes and will not heat pan.
Always disconnect power before opening the chassis.
