Service manual excerpt — CoolAir Portable AC 12k BTU (ac-001)

Model CA-PAC12 · Brand CoolAir · MY2026

If the unit shows error E41 (Compressor high pressure), inspect the Condenser Airflow.
Primary field failure: Condenser Fan Failure. Replace Condenser Fan Motor (CA-CFM-12) when confirmed.
Customer may report: Unit cools weakly and shuts off.
Always disconnect power before opening the chassis.
