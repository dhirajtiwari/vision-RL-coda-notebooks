Service manual excerpt — BreezeTower Bladeless Fan (fan-001)

Model BT-BL60 · Brand BreezeTower · MY2026

If the unit shows error E03 (Motor stall), inspect the Air Multiplier.
Primary field failure: Impeller Bearing Wear. Replace Main Impeller Bearing (BT-IB-60) when confirmed.
Customer may report: Fan oscillates but airflow is weak.
Always disconnect power before opening the chassis.
