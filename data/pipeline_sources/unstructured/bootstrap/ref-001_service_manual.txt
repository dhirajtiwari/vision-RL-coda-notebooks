Service manual excerpt — ChillVault Compact Fridge 180L (ref-001)

Model CV-RF180 · Brand ChillVault · MY2026

If the unit shows error E22 (Defrost timeout), inspect the Defrost System.
Primary field failure: Defrost Heater Failure. Replace Defrost Heater Element (CV-DH-180) when confirmed.
Customer may report: Fridge warm; freezer iced over.
Always disconnect power before opening the chassis.
