Service manual excerpt — FlameMaster Outdoor Grill XL (grill-001)

Model FM-GRXL · Brand FlameMaster · MY2026

If the unit shows error G05 (Igniter no spark), inspect the Ignition System.
Primary field failure: Electronic Igniter Failure. Replace Igniter Electrode Kit (FM-IGN-XL) when confirmed.
Customer may report: Burners will not light.
Always disconnect power before opening the chassis.
